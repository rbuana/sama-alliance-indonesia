Why it works

Pre-aligned, pre-contracted, ready the moment a brief arrives.

01

No competitive conflicts

Each member is the best in their category and non-competing. One PR agency. One performance shop. One tech studio. The best — not the cheapest.

02

One contract, one invoice

The alliance is the commercial entity. You contract once, get one invoice, and hold one party accountable — regardless of how many studios are activated.

03

Shared operating system

All ten agencies use the same briefing templates, project management cadence, and delivery rituals. Onboarding is instant. Ramp-up time is near zero.

04

Founder-led teams

Every member is an independent agency. The people in the room are the founders and principals — not account managers managing delivery elsewhere.

05

Transparent P&L

You see where every dollar goes. Scope, fees, and sub-contractor allocations are shared at the outset — not discovered at reconciliation.

06

Outcome-first culture

Every brief ships with agreed business KPIs. Every quarterly review is honest about what worked, what failed, and what changes. No spin.
